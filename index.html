Vision

"I don't want to just work in neurotechnology — I want to build the companies that define it."

My conviction: invasive BCI will deliver the strongest near-term clinical impact — restoring motor function, treating refractory depression, enabling communication for locked-in patients. But non-invasive technologies will define the field's future as hardware and neural decoding mature. I'm here to help make both happen. Every role I take is a deliberate step toward founding.
